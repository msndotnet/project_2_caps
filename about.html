About Us

History


Brimingham Brims is a family-run business based in Birmingham, England since 1987 - we have gained a reputation as trusted members of the Birmingham retail community. In addition to having our shop in the Heart of Birmingham we have been trading online since 2001.

Quality


The vast majority of our product is made in England we have sought out the top Craftspeople from all over the UK and have a strong working relationship with them. We like to give our customers as much information as possible about their potential purchases. If you have a problem with any of our gifts, we will take them back and offer a replacement or a refund.
